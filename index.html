Samsung Account

What is Samsung Account?

Samsung Account is a free integrated membership service that enables you to use Samsung service on smartphones, tablets, websites, TVs and other devices. Enjoy various Samsung service through your Samsung account, without having to sign up for each service separately..

What are the benefits of me creating an account?

Exclusive offers and perks

Easy order tracking and product registration

Enrollment in the Samsung Rewards program

Apps like, Samsung Pay, and Galaxy Apps

World-class customer support

Products and Orders

How do I place an order

Select an item you would like to purchase, and then click the Buy Now button from either the quick view or product detail page.

You can then choose to continue shopping or go directly to Checkout.

Once you are finished shopping, click on Shopping Cart at the top right corner of page, and follow the Checkout steps.

You will receive an email confirming your order that includes your order number.

How do I find a product online that I saw in a store?

You may use our Search Function to look for an item by keyword or model code.

Return and Cancellations

How and when can I cancel an order?

If your order is eligible for cancellation, a ‘cancel order’ button will be available till the order is delivered. Click this button to cancel your order.
You will be notified regarding status of your cancellation request via Email or you can track the status here www.samsung.com/in/web/my-orders
Please check below the detailed Return & Cancellation Policy

The Warranty provided with Samsung products varies depending on the product. Information regarding the Samsung warranty can be found in the product packaging.
